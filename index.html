Ravi Donepudi

I am a sixth year Ph.D student in the mathematics department at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I am interested in arithmetic geometry, specifically questions involving curves and abelian varieties over fields of positive characteristic. My advisor is Patrick Allen.

Previously, I was an undergraduate student at Colorado College in the Rocky Mountains.
